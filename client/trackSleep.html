Tracking Sleep

Sleep Deprivation can often trigger headaches. If you think you are having headaches due to sleep deprivation you should track your daily sleep.

Tracking time is set to current date and time by default. Change the date and time if required by tapping the date or time showing button. This date time refers to data collection time, not your sleeping time.
